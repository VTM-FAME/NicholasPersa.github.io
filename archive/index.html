The beacon is a body of work archiving points of view (POV) from a 15 mile radius around the Wells Fargo Plaza. As I travel for various reasons around the suburbs, this buidling can be nefariously seen. Living very close to this building means that I feel comforted by its visage. Seeing it towering in the distance signals my home.
